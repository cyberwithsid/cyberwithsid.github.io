About Me

I'm a Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst and GRC specialist with a passion for building proactive cyber defenses. My experience spans real-time threat monitoring, incident response, risk management frameworks, and security automation.

I work across the full security lifecycle — from identifying vulnerabilities and designing GRC frameworks aligned with ISO 27001, NIST, and SOC 2, to hands-on SIEM tuning and threat hunting in Wazuh, Splunk and QRadar.

Beyond operations, I regularly build lab environments, write about blue team tactics, and contribute tools to the infosec community.
